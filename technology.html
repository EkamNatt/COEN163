AI Technology in Waste Management

Our AI technology analyzes your uploaded images to identify various types of waste, facilitating proper recycling and disposal.

How It Works

Step 1: Simply upload an image of your waste, and our AI will analyze it and tell you how to dispose of it correctly. This will be through the use of your frontal camera preferably through your phone.

Step 2: After the image is uploaded, the AI processes it to identify the type of waste. The tool uses visual recognition algorithms to analyze the material composition and shape of the item in the picture.

Step 3: Within seconds, the AI provides the results, classifying the waste type and offering specific instructions on how to properly dispose of or recycle the item. This section might include local recycling guidelines or suggest a drop-off location for items like electronics or hazardous materials.
